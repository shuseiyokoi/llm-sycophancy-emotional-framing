Overall, model behavior varied substantially across prompt treatments. Claude Sonnet 4.5 appeared more tolerant of emotional-identity framing, while Claude Haiku showed more “Yes” on other prompts other than Emotional Identity. Gemini 2.5 Flash kept the same conclusions but showed some confidence shifts, though the sample size was small (n = 10). Gemini Pro produced more “No” responses across treated prompts, especially Emotional Extreme, Emotional Identity, and Emotional Suicidal. GPT-3.5 Turbo increased “No” responses under treatment prompts, while GPT-4o mini showed the strongest reversal, changing its answers nearly 180 degrees across all treatment conditions.
